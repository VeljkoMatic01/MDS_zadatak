About

MDS is an innovative and dynamic platform focused on the capital market, providing users with a unique opportunity to become part of modern financial markets. Our mission is to enhance financial literacy and enable easy access to investment through the most advanced technological solutions.

Through our platform, users can access specialized tools for stock trading, investing, and capital market education. Our vision is to become leaders in providing digital solutions that simplify the investment process, allowing everyone to become part of the global financial system.

The MDS Stock Exchange is characterized by simplicity, security, and transparency, making it an ideal place for every investor, regardless of experience level. Together with our team of experts, we are dedicated to supporting users at every step of their investment journey.

Stock trading on the exchange

How stock trading works on the MDS Stock Exchange?

Buying and selling stocks - On the MDS Stock Exchange, you can easily buy and sell shares of large and well-known companies, using an intuitive interface for fast and secure trading.

Real-time data and analysis - Access the latest market data and analyses in real-time to make informed and efficient investment decisions.

MDS Stocks - Invest in MDS stocks on our platform, offering you a great opportunity to diversify and grow your portfolio.

Automatic purchase at a set value - Our platform enables the automatic purchase of stocks when the price reaches a predefined value, allowing for faster and more efficient transactions.

Thresholds notifications - Track fluctuating margins for your stocks and receive notifications via email or SMS as soon as a critical point is reached, giving you full control over your investments.

Trend tracking and notifications for predefined targets - On the MDS platform, you can monitor current market trends and be notified when stocks reach predefined targets, helping you respond promptly to market changes.
